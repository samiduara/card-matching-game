MEMORY-GAME

Memory Game By Sam Gathoni

The premise of the game is to have a grid of 18 face-down cards. The card faces consist of pairs of matches. Clicking on cards will flip them over, revealing the value. When two are chosen, if it's a match, both cards will disappear. If it's not, the cards will flip back over to face-down. The game should be different every time the game is refreshed.
